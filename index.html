葉劉淑儀 (Regina Ip) 生於新加坡華僑家庭，於聖士提反女子中學及香港大學畢業後，赴英國格拉斯哥大學與美國史丹福大學深造。自 1975 年投身政務主任 (AO) 以來，她見證並參與了香港回歸前後的每一個歷史時刻。

身為前保安局局長，她在推動《基本法》第 23 條立法過程中展現了強韌的政治意志。雖然曾面臨社會爭論與起伏，但她選擇以研究精神重返政壇。創立智庫與政黨，旨在連結專業力量，讓香港在中產繁榮與國家安全之間取得完美平衡。

Born into a Singaporean overseas Chinese family, Regina Ip was educated at St. Stephen's Girls' College and HKU, before pursuing advanced degrees at Glasgow and Stanford. Since joining as an AO in 1975, she has been a pivotal participant in Hong Kong's pre-and-post-handover history.

As former Secretary for Security, she demonstrated iron-willed resolve during the Article 23 legislation process. Despite political turbulence, she returned to the arena with a renewed focus on policy research, founding Savantas and the NPP to bridge the gap between middle-class aspirations and national security.

卓越公職生涯Distinguished Career

1996 - 1998

人民入境事務處處長Director of Immigration

香港歷史上首位女性紀律部隊首長，帶領部門確保 1997 回歸順利。 The first woman to lead a disciplinary force in HK, ensuring a smooth transition during the 1997 Handover.

1998 - 2003

保安局局長Secretary for Security

任內處理無數國家安全與公共秩序事務。2003 年因 23 條立法引發爭議後辭任，赴史丹福進修。 Oversaw national security and public order. Resigned in 2003 after the Article 23 controversy and pursued further studies at Stanford.

2011 - Present

新民黨主席 / 立法會議員Chair of NPP / LegCo Member

連續多屆以高票當選立法會議員。創立新民黨，代表中產及建制派理性的政治聲音。 Elected to LegCo with high vote counts for multiple terms. Founded NPP to represent rational voices of the middle class and pro-establishment.

2022 - Present

行政會議召集人Convenor of Executive Council

出任特區政府最高諮詢機構召集人，成為回歸後首位擔任此職的女性。 Appointed as the head of the top advisory body, the first woman to hold this post since the Handover.

政策觀點Policy Vision

經濟繁榮Economic Prosperity

推動高新科技轉型，鞏固香港作為國際金融與人才中心的地位，並深化「一帶一路」戰略合作。 Advancing tech-innovation while solidifying HK's status as a global financial and talent hub through "Belt & Road" collaborations.

民生與住房Housing & Livelihood

提倡大幅增加土地供應，優化公私營房屋比例，解決中產與基層的居住難題。 Advocating for increased land supply and optimizing public-private housing ratios to solve housing crises for all sectors.

講好香港故事Telling HK Stories

透過「全球繁榮峰會」等平台，主動向世界展示香港在法治下的真實繁榮與活力。 Actively showcasing HK's prosperity and vitality under the rule of law through platforms like the Global Prosperity Summit.
