葉劉淑儀 (Regina Ip) 生於新加坡華僑家庭，於聖士提反女子中學及香港大學畢業後，赴英國格拉斯哥大學與美國史丹福大學深造。自 1975 年投身政務主任 (AO) 以來，她見證並參與了香港回歸前後的每一個歷史時刻。

身為前保安局局長，她在推動《基本法》第 23 條立法過程中展現了強韌的政治意志。雖然曾面臨社會爭論與起伏，但她選擇以研究精神重返政壇。創立智庫與政黨，旨在連結專業力量，讓香港在中產繁榮與國家安全之間取得完美平衡。

Born into a Singaporean overseas Chinese family, Regina Ip was educated at St. Stephen's Girls' College and HKU, before pursuing advanced degrees at Glasgow and Stanford. Since joining as an AO in 1975, she has been a pivotal participant in Hong Kong's pre-and-post-handover history.

As former Secretary for Security, she demonstrated iron-willed resolve during the Article 23 legislation process. Despite political turbulence, she returned to the arena with a renewed focus on policy research, founding Savantas and the NPP to bridge the gap between middle-class aspirations and national security.

完整生平Full Biography

早年生活與教育Early Life & Education

葉劉淑儀於 1950 年 8 月 24 日出生於新加坡華僑家庭，祖籍廣東佛山南海。她在充滿多元文化和國際視野的環境中長大，這對她日後的政治哲學產生了深遠影響。 Born on August 24, 1950, into a Singaporean overseas Chinese family with ancestral roots in Foshan, Guangdong, Regina Ip grew up in a multicultural and internationally-minded environment that profoundly shaped her political philosophy.

她於聖士提反女子中學完成中學教育，隨後升讀香港大學文學院。在大學期間，她以優異成績完成學位，並展現出對公共政策和社會治理的濃厚興趣。 She completed her secondary education at St. Stephen's Girls' College and later studied at the Faculty of Arts at HKU. During her university years, she excelled academically and demonstrated a keen interest in public policy and governance.

大學畢業後，她進一步深造，先後赴英國格拉斯哥大學和美國史丹福大學進修，獲得多個學位。這段海外留學經歷不僅拓寬了她的學術視野，更使她對比較政治制度有了深入的認識。 After graduation, she pursued advanced studies at the University of Glasgow in the UK and Stanford University in the US, earning multiple degrees. This international academic experience broadened her scholarly perspective and deepened her understanding of comparative political systems.

早期公職（1975-1995）Early Career (1975-1995)

1975 年，葉劉淑儀加入港英政府，成為政務主任（Administrative Officer）。作為當時政府中為數不多的女性高級公務員，她面臨著許多挑戰，但憑著卓越的能力和堅定的決心，她逐漸建立了自己的聲譽。 In 1975, Regina Ip joined the British Hong Kong Government as an Administrative Officer. As one of the few senior female civil servants in government at that time, she faced numerous challenges, but through her exceptional abilities and unwavering determination, she gradually established her reputation.

在她的政務主任任期內，她曾在多個不同的政策範疇工作，包括房屋、經濟、教育和社會福利。這些多元的工作經驗使她對香港社會的各個層面都有深入的了解，也為她日後的更高職位奠定了基礎。 During her tenure as AO, she worked across multiple policy areas including housing, economics, education, and social welfare. These diverse experiences gave her in-depth understanding of all aspects of Hong Kong society and laid the groundwork for her future higher positions.

重要職務（1996-2003）Major Positions (1996-2003)

1996 年至 1998 年，葉劉淑儀出任人民入境事務處處長，成為香港歷史上首位女性紀律部隊首長。在她的領導下，入境事務處圓滿完成了香港主權移交的各項準備工作，確保了 1997 年回歸的順利進行。 From 1996 to 1998, Regina Ip served as Director of Immigration, becoming Hong Kong's first female head of a disciplinary force. Under her leadership, the department successfully completed all preparations for Hong Kong's sovereignty transfer, ensuring a smooth handover in 1997.

1998 年至 2003 年，她升任為保安局局長，也是有史以來首位女性保安局局長。在這個位置上，她主要負責香港的國家安全、公共秩序和保安事務。她在任期間，積極推動《基本法》第 23 條的立法，以完善香港的國家安全制度。 From 1998 to 2003, she was appointed Secretary for Security, the first woman to hold this position. In this role, she focused on national security, public order, and security affairs. During her tenure, she actively promoted legislation on Article 23 of the Basic Law to strengthen Hong Kong's national security framework.

政治波折與重返政壇Political Challenges & Political Comeback

2003 年，由於 23 條立法引發社會重大爭議，導致 50 萬人上街遊行反對，葉劉淑儀最終選擇以個人理由辭去保安局局長職務。這次經歷對她而言既是政治挑戰，也是深刻的反思機會。 In 2003, following massive social controversy over Article 23 legislation and a 500,000-person march opposing the bill, Regina Ip resigned as Secretary for Security. This experience was both a political challenge and a profound moment of reflection.

辭職後，她赴美國史丹福大學進行深造和研究，利用這段時間重新思考香港的政治和政策問題。她在學術界發表了多篇論著，闡述她對香港治理和發展的看法。 After her resignation, she pursued further studies and research at Stanford University in the US, using this time to reflect deeply on Hong Kong's political and policy issues. She published several academic works articulating her views on Hong Kong governance and development.

創立新民黨與立法會生涯Founding NPP & Legislative Council Career

2011 年，葉劉淑儀與志同道合的政治伙伴共同創立了新民黨，定位為代表香港中產和建制派的理性聲音。新民黨主張以證據為本的政策制定，反對過度意識形態化，致力於香港的繁榮和穩定。 In 2011, Regina Ip, together with like-minded political partners, founded the New People's Party, positioning itself as the voice of Hong Kong's middle class and pro-establishment. The NPP advocates evidence-based policymaking, opposes excessive ideology, and is committed to Hong Kong's prosperity and stability.

自 2008 年開始，葉劉淑儀以高票當選為香港立法會議員，代表香港島及後來的香港島西選區。她在立法會中表現活躍，提出了許多關於經濟發展、房屋政策、教育改革和法治建設的建議，贏得了中產階層和建制派的廣泛支持。 Since 2008, Regina Ip has been elected to the Legislative Council with high vote counts, representing Hong Kong Island and later the Hong Kong Island West constituency. She has been active in LegCo, proposing numerous initiatives on economic development, housing policy, education reform, and rule of law, earning broad support from the middle class and pro-establishment sectors.

現任行政會議召集人Current Role: Executive Council Convenor

2022 年，葉劉淑儀獲委任為香港特別行政區行政會議召集人，成為香港回歸後首位擔任此職的女性。行政會議是香港特區政府的最高諮詢機構，召集人為政府的主要政策顧問，直接協助行政長官制定政策。 In 2022, Regina Ip was appointed as Convenor of the Executive Council of the HKSAR, the first woman to hold this position since the handover. The Executive Council is the top advisory body of the HKSAR Government, and the Convenor serves as the government's principal policy advisor, directly assisting the Chief Executive in policy formulation.

在這個位置上，她致力於推動香港的經濟發展、民生改善和法治完善。她相信，只有在確保法治和廉政的基礎上，香港才能實現可持續的發展和繁榮。她提倡以理性、實證和尋求最大公約數的方式來處理香港的重大問題。 In this position, she is dedicated to promoting Hong Kong's economic development, improving people's livelihood, and enhancing the rule of law. She believes that Hong Kong can only achieve sustainable development and prosperity on the basis of ensuring rule of law and clean government. She advocates for a rational, evidence-based, and consensus-seeking approach to addressing Hong Kong's major issues.

葉劉淑儀的主要政策立場是什麼？What are Regina Ip's main policy positions?

The party champions rule of law protection, economic development, livelihood improvement, and Hong Kong's stability and prosperity.
